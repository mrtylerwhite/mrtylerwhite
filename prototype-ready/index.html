FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to bring my team?

No. You attend solo. This is a live demonstration: you watch the system run on an example project and learn the method to facilitate it with your team later.

Is this for designers who can't code?

No code required. The workshop is facilitation-first: slide deck, FigJam, Notion, and the ROI calculator. If you can run a stakeholder conversation, you can run Builder Kickoff™.

Do I need FigJam or Notion?

Yes. The FigJam template and Builder Kickoff™ Playbook (including the Notion guides) are core parts of the system. Free tiers work for getting started. The playbook walks you through setup step by step.

Is this just vibe coding?

No. Vibe coding is what you get when there is no structure, no intake, and no idea what you are trying to prove. The result is impressive-looking garbage nobody can ship. This workshop gives you a facilitation system, not a prompt dump.

Will there be a replay?

Yes. Every seat for the July 4 session includes the full replay within 24 hours, so you won't miss the material if something comes up.

Is this beginner friendly?

It assumes you have shipped at least one digital product and know your way around a stakeholder conversation. If you have shipped dozens, it will probably change how you start every new one.

Do I need to prepare anything?

No prep required. I bring the example project. You show up, watch the walkthrough, and leave with the complete Builder Kickoff System™.
